# Surface Grounding — Always Obey

One-page contract for Twin-Sparrow surface work.

> **Hara decides what we remove. Relics shows what remains when Twin-Sparrow is honest on the web.**

```
think-different → product-design → japanese-design → web-design
```

Surface never reorders the value spine. Craft never invents surface doctrine.

---

## Always

1. Run **ex-formation** before decoration (inventory → remove → ma → aliveness).
2. Prefer **hairlines and tone** over boxes and elevation.
3. Prefer **asymmetry (7/5)** over equal portfolio grids when expressive.
4. **One accent** (sage on Relics-class web; status color with meaning on TUI).
5. **Four type voices max** on web; Japanese modern faces for editorial gravity.
6. Motion **explains hierarchy or causal continuity** only; soft blur only as **scrub recede**, never blur-in.
7. Proof is **real** (versions, counts, capabilities) or absent; prefer native material over generic placeholders.
8. Expose **one real operation** (context → orientation → action → verification), not a dashboard of simulated cognition.
9. A structural edge may hand one section into the next when the connection explains continuity.
10. Terminal surfaces remain crisp: full opacity, no blur, no residual transform at maximum scroll.
11. Authored residue is maintained honestly or omitted; never a fake activity feed.
12. Every new section replaces or compresses weaker explanation.
13. Hover is **seijaku**: quiet attention, not spectacle.
14. No Japan motif cosplay; no glass/glow/neon SaaS theater.
15. Greyscale + motion-off still works.

---

## Never (unless explicitly earned and argued)

- Glassmorphism, mesh gradient heroes, glow/bloom
- Blur-in reveals
- Resting-card float shadow stacks
- Fake metrics, fake urgency, fake activity feeds, multi-CTA competition
- Reversible scrub on footers or terminal controls that cannot cross the reading band
- Generic portfolio placeholders when truthful native material exists
- Cloning Relics kinetic deck onto unrelated products as costume
